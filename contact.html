Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I list my fish waste?
Simply register as a supplier, log in to your dashboard, and click "List Fish Waste". Fill in the details about your waste type, quantity, location, and expected price. Industries will see your listing and can contact you directly.
What types of fish waste do you accept?
We accept all types of fish waste including heads, tails, scales, bones, guts, and mixed waste. Any part of the fish that's typically discarded can be listed on our platform.
How do I get paid?
Payment is arranged directly between suppliers and industries. BIOFLOW facilitates the connection and can help coordinate logistics, but transactions are handled between the parties involved.
Is there a fee to use BIOFLOW?
Registration and listing are completely free for suppliers. Industries pay a small commission only on successful transactions. We believe in making it easy for everyone to participate in the circular economy.
Do you handle transportation?
We can connect you with logistics partners who specialize in transporting fish waste. You can also arrange your own transportation. Our platform makes it easy to coordinate pickup and delivery schedules.
