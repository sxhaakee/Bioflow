About BIOFLOW

Transforming fish waste into opportunities, one connection at a time

Our Story

BIOFLOW was born from a simple observation: millions of tons of fish waste are discarded every year, polluting our oceans and wasting valuable resources. Meanwhile, industries desperately need these materials for fertilizers, animal feed, and other products.

We saw an opportunity to solve both problems simultaneously. By creating a platform that connects fish waste suppliers with industries that need it, we're turning environmental pollution into economic opportunity.

Today, BIOFLOW serves hundreds of suppliers and industries across India, facilitating the recycling of thousands of tons of fish waste and generating millions in revenue for coastal communities.

Mission & Vision

Driving sustainable change through innovation

Our Mission

To create a sustainable ecosystem where fish waste is recognized as a valuable resource, not garbage. We connect suppliers with industries, reduce ocean pollution, and generate income for coastal communities while supporting the circular economy.

Our Vision

A world where zero fish waste ends up in oceans or landfills. Every kilogram is recycled into valuable products, creating a thriving circular economy that benefits both people and the planet.
