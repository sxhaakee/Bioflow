Why Fish Waste Matters

Understanding the problem, discovering the solution, and creating a sustainable future together

The Problem

  • 2.3 Million Tons of fish waste generated annually in India alone
  • Ocean Pollution: Most fish waste is dumped back into oceans, causing water pollution and harming marine life
  • Health Hazards: Rotting fish waste near markets creates foul odors and spreads diseases
  • Lost Revenue: Fishermen and markets lose potential income by treating waste as garbage
  • Resource Waste: Valuable nutrients and proteins are wasted instead of being utilized
